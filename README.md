# Aadhaar Biometric Update Preparedness Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes UIDAI Aadhaar biometric and demographic update datasets to identify districts with low preparedness for the mandatory biometric update required at age 15. The study proposes targeted awareness and outreach interventions to improve compliance and service accessibility.

## Problem Statement
Biometric updates are mandatory for Aadhaar holders at age 15 to ensure authentication reliability. However, district level disparities in update activity indicate gaps in awareness and access. This project aims to identify such districts and recommend data driven intervention strategies.

## Datasets Used
Aadhaar Biometric Update Dataset
Aadhaar Demographic Update Dataset
Both datasets were aggregated at the district level and cleaned before analysis.

## Methodology
Data cleaning and preprocessing
District level aggregation of age wise transactions
Calculation of biometric preparedness metric
Supporting analysis using demographic update engagement
Risk based district classification

## Key Insights
Significant inter district variation in biometric update preparedness
Several districts exhibit low adolescent update activity
Low biometric preparedness often correlates with low demographic engagement

## Recommendations
School linked biometric update awareness camps
Mobile Aadhaar enrolment units in high risk districts
Pre age 15 outreach through educational institutions
District level monitoring of biometric update preparedness
